Protect yourself from Coinbase Card scams

  • Never pay to get your card. Coinbase Card is free. Any message asking for a fee to unlock or ship your card is fraudulent.
  • Never share your card number with Coinbase "support." Real Coinbase support never needs your full card number or CVV.
  • Ignore social media DMs. Coinbase does not offer card support through Twitter/X, Instagram, or Telegram.
  • Only activate through the official app. Do not follow activation links sent via email or text that you did not initiate.

Card FAQ

Everything you need to know about the Coinbase Card

EligibilityIs the Coinbase Card free?
›

Yes — completely free. There are no application fees, no annual fees, no monthly fees, and no foreign transaction fees. Some third-party ATM operators charge their own withdrawal fees, but Coinbase does not add any fee on top of those.

EligibilityWho can get the Coinbase Card?
›

You must be:

  • A US resident (18 years or older)
  • A verified Coinbase account holder (identity verification completed)
  • Located in an eligible state — check the app for your state's status
No credit check is performed. Your credit score is not affected by applying.
ApplicationHow long does approval take?
›

Most applications are reviewed instantly. In some cases, additional review may take up to 3–5 business days. You'll receive a notification in the app when a decision is made.

Once approved, your virtual card is available immediately while your physical card ships in 7–10 business days.

ApplicationIs a credit check required?
›

No. The Coinbase Card is a debit card linked to your Coinbase account balance — not a line of credit. No credit check is run, and your credit score is not impacted in any way.

RewardsHow do crypto rewards work?
›

Every time you make an eligible purchase with your Coinbase Card, you earn a percentage of that purchase back as crypto. The reward is calculated based on the USD amount spent and credited as crypto at the market rate at time of reward issuance.

RewardsWhen are rewards deposited?
›

Rewards are typically deposited to your Coinbase account within a few days after each eligible purchase. You can view your reward history under Card → Rewards in the app.

RewardsCan I change my rewards asset?
›

Yes — and it's free. Go to Card → Rewards → Change reward in the app. Changes take effect on your next eligible purchase. There's no limit to how often you can change it.

RewardsWhich purchases don't earn rewards?
›

The following transactions typically do not earn rewards:

  • ATM withdrawals
  • Cash advances
  • Peer-to-peer transactions (e.g. Venmo, PayPal)
  • Gift card purchases at certain retailers
  • Purchases later returned or reversed
SpendingHow does crypto get converted when I pay?
›

At the moment of purchase, Coinbase automatically sells crypto from your selected spending wallet at the current market rate and pays the merchant in USD. You never need to manually convert before spending.

Each conversion is a taxable event in the US. See the Taxes tab for more information.

Limits are subject to change. Your available balance also acts as a limit — you can only spend what's in your account.

SpendingWhich crypto can I spend?
›

You can set any supported crypto in your Coinbase account as your spending asset. Commonly used assets include:

  • Bitcoin (BTC)
  • Ethereum (ETH)
  • USD Coin (USDC)
  • Stellar (XLM), and more

USDC is a popular choice since it's a stablecoin pegged to USD, reducing the impact of price swings at time of purchase.

Card ManagementHow do I freeze or unfreeze my card?
›

Open the Coinbase app → tap Card → tap Freeze card. Your card is locked instantly — no purchases can be made while frozen. Tap Unfreeze to restore it at any time.

Freezing your card does not cancel it and does not affect your rewards or account.
Card ManagementMy card is lost or stolen — what do I do?
›
  1. Freeze the card immediately — Card tab → Freeze card
  2. Report it — Card settings → Report lost or stolen
  3. A replacement card will be mailed at no cost within 7–10 business days
  4. Your virtual card continues to work while you wait
If you see unauthorized charges, also contact support via Settings → Help → Contact us immediately.
Card ManagementHow do I cancel my card?
›

Go to Card → Card settings → Close card in the app. This permanently cancels your card. You can reapply in the future if you change your mind.

Card ManagementCan I use it with Apple Pay or Google Pay?
›

Yes. Add your Coinbase Card to Apple Wallet or Google Wallet through the app. Once added, you can tap to pay at any contactless terminal and use it for in-app purchases.

To add: Card → Virtual card → Add to Apple/Google Wallet

⚠️ Scam AlertWill Coinbase ever ask for my card number?
›

No. Coinbase support will never ask for your:

  • Full card number
  • CVV / security code
  • Card PIN
  • One-time passwords or 2FA codes
If anyone claiming to be Coinbase asks for these, it is a scam. Hang up or end the chat and report it via the app immediately.
⚠️ Scam AlertSomeone said I need to pay to activate my card — is that real?
›
This is 100% a scam. Coinbase never charges a fee to apply for, ship, activate, or unblock your Coinbase Card.

Scammers impersonate Coinbase support and claim you need to pay a "processing fee," "tax," or "activation fee" to get your card. These are fraudulent. Report the interaction to:

  • Coinbase support — Settings → Help → Contact us
  • The FTC — reportfraud.ftc.gov
SecurityHow is my card protected against fraud?
›

Your Coinbase Card includes multiple fraud protections:

  • Instant freeze — lock the card in one tap if you see suspicious activity
  • Real-time transaction alerts — get notified for every purchase
  • Zero liability policy — you're not responsible for unauthorized purchases when reported promptly (per Visa's Zero Liability Policy)
  • EMV chip — chip card technology makes in-person fraud harder
TaxesAre crypto card purchases taxable?
›

Yes. In the United States, using the Coinbase Card to make a purchase triggers a crypto-to-USD conversion, which is a taxable event under IRS guidance. This means:

  • Each purchase is treated as if you sold the crypto for USD
  • Capital gains or losses apply based on your cost basis
  • This applies even to small, everyday purchases
Using a stablecoin like USDC typically has minimal or no tax impact since the price doesn't change — but consult a tax professional for your situation.
TaxesAre crypto rewards taxable?
›

Generally, yes. The IRS treats crypto rewards received as taxable income at the fair market value on the date received. When you later sell the reward crypto, capital gains rules apply.

Coinbase provides a tax report in your account under Taxes → Documents to help you track your activity.

TaxesWhere can I download my transaction history?
›

Go to coinbase.com → Taxes → Documents or in the app under Profile → Taxes. You can download transaction history as a CSV file and access your annual gain/loss reports.

Coinbase partners with TurboTax and CoinTracker to simplify crypto tax filing. Look for import options in your tax software.
