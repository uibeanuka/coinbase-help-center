Protect yourself from Coinbase Card scams

  • Never pay to get your card. Coinbase Card is free. Any message asking for a fee to unlock or ship your card is fraudulent.
  • Never share your card number with Coinbase "support." Real Coinbase support never needs your full card number or CVV.
  • Ignore social media DMs. Coinbase does not offer card support through Twitter/X, Instagram, or Telegram.
  • Only activate through the official app. Do not follow activation links sent via email or text that you did not initiate.
