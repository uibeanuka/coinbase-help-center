Never share your recovery phrase

Anyone asking for your 12‑word recovery phrase — including someone claiming to be Coinbase Support — is a scammer. Coinbase staff will never ask for it.

📞
Unsolicited calls? Hang up.

Coinbase does not make unsolicited phone calls. If you receive one, it is a scam. Report it via the app immediately.
